Gautam Adani

Gautam Adani is an Indian industrialist and the founder of the Adani Group, a multinational conglomerate involved in ports, airports, power generation, renewable energy, logistics, and infrastructure development. His journey from a modest background to building one of India’s largest business empires is a source of inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs.
